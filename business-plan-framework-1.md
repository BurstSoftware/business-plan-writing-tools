# Rapid Business Plan Preparation

## When You're Really, Really in a Hurry

Sometimes there isn't enough time to prepare a fully detailed business plan. In these situations, focus on the information investors care about most.

---

## Investor Critical Issues

### 1. The Business Concept

Clearly explain:

- What the business does
- What problem it solves
- Why it exists

---

### 2. The Size and Nature of the Market

Describe:

- Market size
- Customer demand
- Industry opportunity

---

### 3. What's Happening with the Competition

Identify:

- Major competitors
- Competitive advantages
- Market positioning

---

### 4. What Kind of Growth You Project

Include:

- Revenue projections
- Customer growth
- Expansion plans

---

### 5. Who's on Your Team

Introduce:

- Founders
- Leadership
- Advisors
- Key employees

---

Concentrate on addressing these issues first.

# Quick Investor Checklist

Before presenting your business plan, ensure you can clearly answer:

- What is the business?
- Who is the customer?
- How large is the market?
- Who are the competitors?
- Why is your solution better?
- How will the business grow?
- Who is leading the company?
- How much funding is needed?
- How will investors make a return?
